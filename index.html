Our Method

How we work
with your farm.

A repeatable, science-backed framework applied to every farm we work with. No shortcuts. No guesswork.

01
Assessment
We come to you first.

On-site farm visit, soil sampling from multiple grid points, and a full farmer interview to understand history, crop patterns, and ambitions.

02
Analysis
Science in the lab.

All samples processed in NABL accredited laboratories. Full nutrient panel, organic matter content, microbial load, and water retention capacity.

03
Prescription
A plan, not a report.

Detailed, season-by-season action plan written in plain language. Covers soil amendments, crop choices, irrigation, and input scheduling.

04
Monitoring
We track. We adapt.

Quarterly field reviews, yield tracking, and annual re-analysis. We adjust as conditions change — droughts, pest pressure, market shifts.

About Terrava

Built by agronomists
for farmers first.

Terrava Agro was founded by a team of agronomists and soil scientists who had spent years watching good farmland deteriorate through over-cultivation, poor input practices, and lack of professional guidance.

Our name comes from the land itself — terra, earth. We believe every field holds the chemistry and biology it needs to recover. Our role is to read it correctly, and then prescribe with precision.
