What We Do

Three ways we revive your land.

Every engagement begins with the soil. We look, measure, and then prescribe — never the other way around.

01
Soil Health
Dead soil brought

Full laboratory analysis of organic carbon, nitrogen, phosphorus, pH, and microbial density. We identify exactly what your soil is lacking — and prescribe a restoration programme with measurable milestones. Results tracked over two complete crop cycles.

Our Method

How we work
with your farm.

01
Assessment
We come to you first.

On-site farm visit, soil sampling from multiple grid points, and a full farmer interview to understand history, crop patterns, and ambitions.

02
Analysis
Science in the lab.

All samples processed in ICAR-affiliated labs. Full nutrient panel, organic matter content, microbial load, and water retention capacity.

03
Prescription
A plan, not a report.

Detailed, season-by-season action plan written in plain language. Covers soil amendments, crop choices, irrigation, and input scheduling.

04
Monitoring
We track. We adapt.

Quarterly field reviews, yield tracking, and annual re-analysis. We adjust the programme as conditions change — droughts, pest pressure, or market shifts.

About Terrava

Built by agronomists
for farmers first.

Terrava Agro was founded in 2016 by a team of agronomists and soil scientists who had spent years watching good farmland deteriorate through over-cultivation, poor input practices, and lack of professional guidance.

Our name comes from the land itself — terra, earth. We believe every field holds the chemistry and biology it needs to recover. Our role is to read it correctly, and then prescribe with precision.
